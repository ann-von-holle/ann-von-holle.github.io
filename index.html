Ann Von Holle

Associate Research Director

I am an epidemiologist with work experience spanning fields of molecular diagnostic test quality, breast cancer epidemiology, and biomarkers. My doctoral and postdoctoral experiences enabled me to grow my experience in research conceptualization, writing, mentoring, and advanced modeling.

Interests

  • Molecular diagnostic test quality assessment
  • Biomarkers in cancer and chronic disease epidemiology
  • Longitudinal, time-to-event, and structural equation modeling analyses
  • Data visualization
  • Reproducible research

Education
